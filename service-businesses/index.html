A few straight answers

Questions you're probably asking.

I'm not techie at all. Is this really for me?

That's exactly who I build for. You never touch the wiring. I do. You get plain-English explanations and a system that simply runs. Being non-techie is a reason to do this, not a reason to avoid it.

What tools do you work with?

GoHighLevel is my home base, so most systems I build live there. When your business needs something of its own, I build a custom app instead. Either way you get one system made to fit how you actually work, not a pile of tools you have to manage.

Will automated messages make my business feel robotic?

The opposite. Done well, automation catches the leads and reminders you were dropping, so clients hear back faster and feel looked after. Every message still goes out in your voice, on your terms, with no fake urgency or pressure tricks.

How long does setup take?

Most systems are mapped in days and live within 2 to 3 weeks, depending on scope. We build the highest-impact pieces first, usually lead follow-up and reminders, so you feel the results early rather than waiting for everything at once.

What does it cost?

It depends on what we're building, but projects usually start around $2,000. We'll pin down exact numbers on the call, once I understand what your system actually needs.

What if something breaks later?

After every build I offer a free 14-day support window, so if any bugs turn up I fix them at no extra cost. Everything is documented and tested too, and I don't disappear after handoff, so I'm reachable when your business grows and the system needs to grow with it.
